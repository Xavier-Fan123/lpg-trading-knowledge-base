油轮航次租船合同简介

Asbatankvoy 为最常用的格式合同，被广泛应用根据实际情况，船东和租家经常会对格式合同进行修改、补充目前Sinochem Standard Terms April 2008、Sinochem Clauses April 2008、Unipec Special Provisions April 2008也被广泛应用，同时还会加入Additional Clauses进行补充附加条款的效力优先于格式条款航次租船合同的条款可归类为一下几类：介绍条款、船舶说明、预备航次、航次条款、货物条款、港口货物装卸、安全港口与泊位、装卸时间和滞期费条款、运费、亏舱费和其它费用、保护性条款、留置权、互有责任碰撞、新杰森、共同海损、提单、罢工、战争等。

Asbatankvoy Part II – 保证条款

1. WARRANTY—­VOYAGE—CARGOThe vessel, classed as specified in Part I hereof, and to be so maintained during the currency of this Charter, shall, with all convenient dispatch, proceed as ordered to Loading Port(s) named in accordance with Clause 4 hereof, or so near thereunto as she may safely get (always afloat), and being seaworthy, and having all pipes, pumps and heater coils in good working order, and being in every respect fitted for the voyage, so far as the foregoing conditions can be attained by the exercise of due diligence, perils of the sea and any other cause of whatsoever kind beyond the Owner's and/or Master's control excepted, shall load (always afloat), from the factors of the Charterer a full and complete cargo of petroleum and/or its products in bulk, not exceeding what she can reasonably stow and carry over and above her bunker fuel, consumable stores, boiler feed, culinary and drinking water, and complement and their effects (sufficient space to be left in the tanks to provide for the expansion of the cargo), and being so loaded shall forthwith proceed, as ordered on signing Bills of Lading, direct to the Discharging Port(s), or so near thereunto as she may safely get (always afloat), and deliver said cargo. If heating of the cargo is requested by the Charterer, the Owner shall exercise due diligence to maintain the temperatures requested.本条款为保证条款，对船舶，航程，货物做了一个整体的要求。本船具有第I 部分所述船级，并应在本租约期间保持该船级，本船应以合理速度驶往依据本租约第4 条指定的装货港或其附近本船所能安全抵达（始终处于浮泊状态）的地点。本船适航，各种输油管、泵及加热管系处于良好工作状态，并在各方面适合本航次。经恪尽职责能满足前述条件的范围内，船东和／或船长无法控制的海上风险及任何其他原因除外。本船应（在始终浮泊状态下）装载由承租人的代理人提供的满舱满载的散装石油和／或石油制品，但不超过除去燃油、应耗物料、锅炉用料、蔬菜及饮用水、船上定员及其财产（并为货物膨胀留出充分的货舱舱容）后本船所能合理载运的范围。如此装载后，本船应按所签提单规定的顺序，直接驶往卸货港或其附近本船所能安全抵达（始终处于浮泊状态）的地点交付上述货物。如承租人要求对货物进行加温，船东应恪尽职责保持货物在所要求的温度。

Asbatankvoy Part II -- 运费

2. FREIGHT.Freight shall be at the rate stipulated in Part I and shall be computed on intake quantity (except deadfreight as per Clause 3) as shown on the Inspector's Certificate of Inspection. Payment of freight shall be made by Charterer without discount upon delivery of cargo at destination, less any disbursements or advances made to the Master or Owner's agents at ports of loading and/or discharge and cost of insurance thereon. No deduction of freight shall be made for water and/or sediment contained in the cargo. The services of the Petroleum Inspector shall be arranged and paid for by the Charterer who shall furnish the Owner with a copy of the Inspector's Certificate.基于检验人的检验证书上船舶装载的货物数量（第3 条约定的亏舱费除外）以及第I 部分约定的费率计算运费。承租人应在货物于卸货港交付后支付未作扣减的运费，但可扣除在装货港和/或卸货港为船长或船东的代理支付的任何费用或垫付费用以及关于此的保险费用。运费不应以货物中含有水分和/或沉淀物而作扣减。石油检验人服务应由承租人安排，承租人并承担相应的费用，同时应提供给船东一份检验人证书副本。运费一般用BL中Gross量来算，包含货油中的水杂。当BL中只有NET量时用装港的数量证书为准。

Asbatankvoy Part II – 亏舱费

3. DEADFREIGHT.Should the Charterer fail to supply a full cargo, the Vessel may, at the Master's option, and shall, upon request of the Charterer, proceed on her voyage, provided that the tanks in which cargo is loaded are sufficiently filled to put her in seaworthy condition. In that event, however, deadfreight shall be paid at the rate specified in Part I hereof on the difference between the intake quantity and the quantity the Vessel would have carried if loaded to her minimum permissible freeboard for the voyage.当承租人未能提供充足的货物，船长有权选择船舶按原定航次继续执行，如承租人要求船舶执行原定航次，船舶应如此执行，但前提是载货船舱中已经装载了足使本船处于适航状态的货物。在前述情形下，亏舱费应当被支付，费率为第I 部分中约定的费率，数量为装货数量和本船达到本航次最低允许干舷高度时所能载运的数量之差亏舱费：本条款已经演变为按照最低货量计算运费。

Asbatankvoy Part II -- Freight 运费

WS全称 WORLDWIDE TANKER NOMINAL FREIGHT SCALE （世界油船名义运价费率表），该费率表列明WS100所对应的油轮各航线的基本运费率（flat rate），每年调整一次，根据当年的港口费，燃料费，运河费等运营成本调整。 WS指数 指市场运费与WS所规定的基本运费率的百分比，反映了即期市场油轮的运费价格水平。WS指数的制定依据是以一艘 航速为14.5节的7.5万吨级油船，在最经济条件下运行时盈亏平衡时的收入，定为WS指数基准100。越大的船舶基于规模效应，每吨货物的运费应当会越低，因此，VLCC 船舶在租约中约定的费率常常远远低于WS100所有费率均按去航满载、回程空载的往返航次计算

Asbatankvoy Part II -- Freight 运费

油轮运费计算实例1. 油轮计价运费：由最低货量（计费吨）、基本费率（Flat Rate）、和合同双方拟定的WS运价指数三部分组成：运费=计费吨 x 基本费率（Flat Rate） x 运价指数WS%例如：航线Ras Tanura -- Ningbo，查询WS网站得flat rate 24，合同约定WS65，minimum 260,000mt货量<=260,000mt时，Freight = 260,000mt x 24 x 65%货量>260,000mt时，比如货量270,000mt，overage 10,000mtFreight = 260,000mt x 24 x 65% + 10,000mt x 24 x 65% x 50%2. 包干运费（Lumpsum）多用于加勒比、巴拿马航线；北海，远东等航线

Asbatankvoy Part II -- 指定装港及临时改港

4. NAMING LOADING AND DISCHARGE PORTS.(a)   The Charterer shall name the loading port or ports at least twenty­four (24) hours prior to the Vessel's readiness to sail from the last previous port of discharge, or from bunkering port for the voyage, or upon signing this Charter if the Vessel has already sailed. However, Charterer shall have the option of ordering the Vessel to the following destinations for wireless orders:(b)  If lawful and consistent with Part I and with the Bills of Lading, the Charterer shall have the option of nominating a discharging port or ports by radio to the Master on or before the Vessel's arrival at or off the following places:(c)   Any extra expense incurred in connection with any change in loading or discharging ports (so named) shall be paid for by the Charterer and any time thereby lost to the Vessel shall count as used laytime.(a) 承租人应在船舶离开上一个卸港或本航次的加油港前24 小时指定装港，如在签订本合同时船舶已经在航行中，则应当即指定装货港。然而，承租人有权通过无线电指定命令船舶前往以下目的地。(b) 在合法，并和第I 部分、提单约定相符的情形下，承租人有权在船舶抵达如下地点时或在此之前，用无线电通知船长所指定的一个或多个卸货港。(c) 承租人应当支付更改装货港或卸货港（已指定）所引起的任何相关额外费用，任何因此损失的时间应当计为装卸时间。

Asbatankvoy Part II -- Laycan 受载期和解约日

5. LAYDAYS.Laytime shall not commence before the date stipulated in Part I, except with the Charterer's sanction. Should the Vessel not be ready to load by 4:00 o'clock P.M. (local time) on the cancelling date stipulated in Part I, the Charterer shall have the option of cancelling this Charter by giving Owner notice of such cancellation within twenty­four (24) hours after such cancellation date; otherwise this Charter to remain in full force and effect.Sinochem standard terms clause 5：If the vessel has not given Notice of Readiness to load by 1600 hours local time on the cancelling date, laytime shall commence upon the vessel's arrival in berth or 48 hours after nor tendered,whichever occurs first.if it becomes evident that the ship will miss cancelling, owner to give a new eta and laycan which charterers must either confirm or deecline within 72 hours.if charterer elects to confirm then the c/p remains in full effect with new laycan. if charterer elects to decline,then c/p to be cancelled without recourse to either party'.合同中一般规定laycan为2-3天，最终由租家narrow down到一天(在voyage order中写明）

Asbatankvoy Part II -- laytime装卸时间

6. NOTICE OF READINESS.Upon arrival at customary anchorage at each port of loading or discharge, the Master or his agent shall give the Charterer or his agent notice by letter, telegraph, wireless or telephone that the Vessel is ready to load or discharge cargo, berth or no berth, and laytime, as hereinafter provided, shall commence upon the expiration of six (6) hours after receipt of such notice, or upon the Vessel's arrival in berth (i.e., finished mooring when at a sealoading or discharging terminal and all fast when loading or discharging alongside a wharf), whichever first occurs. However, where delay is caused to Vessel getting into berth after giving notice of readiness for any reason over which Charterer has no control, such delay shall not count as used laytime.Laytime起算时间为NOR+6或靠泊时间，两者以较早为准。靠泊时间即all fast/finished mooring

Asbatankvoy Part II -- laytime装卸时间

7. HOURS FOR LOADING AND DISCHARGING.The number of running hours specified as laytime in Part I shall be permitted the Charterer as laytime for loading and discharging cargo; but any delay due to the Vessel's condition or breakdown or inability of the Vessel's facilities to load or discharge cargo within the time allowed shall not count as used laytime. If regulations of the Owner or port authorities prohibit loading or discharging of the cargo at night, time so lost shall not count as used laytime; if the Charterer, shipper or consignee prohibits loading or discharging at night, time so lost shall count as used laytime. Time consumed by the vessel in moving from loading or discharge port anchorage to her loading or discharge berth, discharging ballast water or slops, will not count as used laytime.Addition：MAX 3 hours waitting for loading documents in loading port(s) to be for Owners account, thereafter to be for charterers.本条款规定了laytime停算的情况，主要是指由船东有责任的过错所引起的延误及港口规定引起的延误可以停算laytime。从锚地到泊位这段时间应从laytime中扣除。完货后等待文件（空距报告、数量证书等）的时间为租家“免费”三小时

Asbatankvoy Part II -- laytime装卸时间

8. DEMURRAGE.Charterer shall pay demurrage per running hour and pro rata for a part thereof at the rate specified in Part I for all time that loading and discharging and used laytime as elsewhere herein provided exceeds the allowed laytime elsewhere herein specified. If, however, demurrage shall be incurred at ports of loading and/or discharge by reason of fire, explosion, storm or by a strike, lockout, stoppage or restraint of labor or by breakdown of machinery or equipment in or about the plant of the Charterer, supplier, shipper or consignee of the cargo, the rate of demurrage shall be reduced one­half of the amount stated in Part I per running hour or pro rate for part of an hour for demurrage so incurred. The Charterer shall not be liable for any demurrage for delay caused by strike, lockout, stoppage or restraint of labor for Master, officers and crew of the Vessel or tugboat or pilots.由于装港和/或卸港的火灾、爆炸、风暴或因为承租人、供货人、发货人或收货人的工厂内外的罢工、停业、停工或劳动力限制或机器设备的故障所致，滞期费算一半。由于船舶或拖轮的船长、高级船员和船员，或引水员罢工、停业、停工或劳动力限制所引起的延误而产生的任何滞期费，承租人不应当承担。

Asbatankvoy Part II -- laytime装卸时间

9. SAFE BERTHING­SHIFTING.The vessel shall load and discharge at any safe place or wharf, or alongside vessels or lighters reachable on her arrival, which shall be designated and procured by the Charterer, provided the Vessel can proceed thereto, lie at, and depart therefrom always safely afloat, any lighterage being at the expense, risk and peril of the Charterer. The Charterer shall have the right of shifting the Vessel at ports of loading and/or discharge from one safe berth to another on payment of all towage and pilotage shifting to next berth, charges for running lines on arrival at and leaving that berth, additional agency charges and expense, customs overtime and fees, and any other extra port charges or port expenses incurred by reason of using more than one berth. Time consumed on account of shifting shall count as used laytime except as otherwise provided in Clause 15.本条款适用于STS和二次靠泊的情况，过驳费用由租家承担。泊位间移泊费用由租家承担，包括拖轮、引航、额外代理费、系解锚费，边防费（immigration）、油围栏费（oil fense）等。时间连续计入laytime，不扣减。

Asbatankvoy Part II -- 泵入和泵出

10. PUMPING IN AND OUT.The cargo shall be pumped into the Vessel at the expense, risk and peril of the Charterer, and shall be pumped out of the Vessel at the expense of the Vessel, but at the risk and peril of the Vessel only so far as the Vessel's permanent hose connections, where delivery of the cargo shall be taken by the Charterer or its consignee. If required by Charterer, Vessel after discharging is to clear shore pipe lines of cargo by pumping water through them and time consumed for this purpose shall apply against allowed laytime. The Vessel shall supply her pumps and the necessary power for discharging in all ports, as well as necessary hands. However, should the Vessel be prevented from supplying such power by  reason of  regulations prohibiting fires on  board, the Charterer or consignee shall supply, at its expense, all power necessary for discharging as well as loading, but the Owner shall pay for power supplied to the Vessel for other purposes. If cargo is loaded from lighters, the Vessel shall furnish steam at Charterer's expense for pumping cargo into its Vessel, if requested by the Charterer, providing the Vessel has facilities for generating steam and is permitted to have fires on board. All overtime of officers and crew incurred in loading and/or discharging shall be for account of the Vessel.泵入的费用和风险由租家承担，泵出的由船东承担但仅限于输油管连接处若租家要求，在卸货后从船上向岸上输油管泵水以清洗输油管，所用时间计入laytime本船在各港口应提供卸货所用泵和必要的动力及人员。但如有关规则禁止船上明火而使本船无法提供此种动力，则承租人或收货人应提供卸货以及装货所需动力并承担费用，但为其它用途提供给本船的动力的费用，应由船东支付。如货物自驳船上装入本船，如承租人要求本船提供将货物泵入本船所需的蒸汽，当本船具有产生蒸汽之设备且允许在船上明火，本船应当提供，其费用由承租人承担。在装货和／或卸货期间高级船员、船员的加班费应由本船承担。

Asbatankvoy Part II -- laytime装卸时间

11. HOSES: MOORING AT SEA TERMINALS.Hoses for loading and discharging shall be furnished by the Charterer and shall be connected and disconnected by the Charterer, or, at the option of the Owner, by the Owner at the Charterer's risk and expense. Laytime shall continue until the hoses have been disconnected. When Vessel loads or discharges at a sea terminal, the Vessel shall be properly equipped at Owner's expense for loading or discharging at such place, including suitable ground tackle, mooring lines and equipment for handling submarine hoses.系泊于海上泊位装货和卸货所需管路应由承租人提供并负责连接和拆除，或者，船东可选择由船东提供、负责连接和摘除，但风险和费用由承租人承担。本条款规定了laytime的终止以为拆管时间为准。如本船在海上泊位装货或卸货，本船应具有在此种地点装货或卸货所需的适当设备，包括适当的锚泊属具、系泊缆绳和水下输油管路操作设备，船东承担前述设备的费用。
